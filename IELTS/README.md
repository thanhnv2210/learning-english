# IELTS — Goal: Band 6.5

Purpose

This folder contains a focused study plan, templates, and practice prompts to help you reach an IELTS overall band of 6.5, with emphasis on speaking and writing for technical professionals.

How to use

- Follow the 12-week plan in `plan.md` and log weekly progress in `IELTS/logs/`.
- Use `IELTS/practice-prompts.md` for timed speaking and writing drills.
- Use `IELTS/templates/` for speaking and writing self-review templates.

Targets & scoring

- Aim: overall 6.5 with minimum 6.0 in each module.
- Track mock test scores in `IELTS/logs/mock-scores.md`.

Recommended materials

- Cambridge IELTS series (practice tests)
- Short speaking topic lists (online)
- Podcasts and TED talks for listening and vocabulary

Folder structure

- `IELTS/plan.md` — 12-week study plan
- `IELTS/practice-prompts.md` — prompts for Speaking/Writing
- `IELTS/templates/` — templates for self-review and scoring
- `IELTS/logs/` — (create) weekly logs and mock score tracking
