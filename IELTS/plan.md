# IELTS 12-Week Plan (target Band 6.5)

Overview

- 12 weeks, focused on speaking and writing improvement for technical professionals.
- Weekly cadence: 5 practice days, 2 review days.

Weekly structure (example week)

- Day 1: Timed Speaking Task (2–3 min) + self-review
- Day 2: Writing Task 1 practice (20 min) + model answer review
- Day 3: Vocabulary & Listening (podcast + notes)
- Day 4: Timed Speaking Task (part 2 style, 2 min) + pronunciation drills
- Day 5: Writing Task 2 (40 min) + feedback
- Day 6: Peer review OR teacher mock (optional)
- Day 7: Rest and review recordings

Progress checks

- Every 4th week: Full mock test (all modules) and record scores in `IELTS/logs/mock-scores.md`.
- Keep a weekly log in `IELTS/logs/YYYY-WW.md` with notes and action items.

Tips for tech professionals

- Practice explaining technical concepts simply — this improves coherence and lexical choice.
- Use PR explanations and design docs as writing practice: convert them to Task 1/Task 2 styles.
- Record and time yourself; aim for consistent pacing and fewer fillers.
