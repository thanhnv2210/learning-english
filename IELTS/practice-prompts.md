# IELTS Practice Prompts (Speaking & Writing)

Speaking — Part 1 (introductory)

- "Tell me about your current job. What do you do?"
- "How do you usually spend your weekends?"

Speaking — Part 2 (long turn, 2 minutes)

- "Describe a project you are proud of. You should say: what the project was, your role, challenges you faced, and explain why you are proud of it."
- "Describe a technical problem you solved recently. Explain the problem, your approach, and the outcome."

Speaking — Part 3 (discussion)

- "How important is clear communication in software teams?"
- "What are the challenges of explaining technical issues to non-technical stakeholders?"

Writing — Task 1 (Academic-like for tech)

- "The chart below shows the number of API requests handled by three services over 6 months. Summarize the main trends and compare the services."
- "A company recorded monthly deployment failures for 12 months. Describe the key trends."

Writing — Task 2 (Essay)

- "Some say that software engineers should spend more time writing documentation than coding. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
- "Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of remote work for software development teams."

Practice tips

- Time yourself strictly (Part 2: 2 minutes; Task 1: 20 min; Task 2: 40 min).
- Record speaking tasks; listen and note 3 improvements.
- For writing, write an outline first (5 min), then draft.
