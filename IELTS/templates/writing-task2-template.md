# Writing Task 2 Template

Structure:
1. Introduction: Paraphrase the question and state your position.
2. Body paragraph 1: Main idea + example
3. Body paragraph 2: Counterpoint or second idea + example
4. Conclusion: Summarize and restate position

Timing:
- Spend 5–7 minutes planning, 30–35 minutes writing, 3–5 minutes proofreading.

Tips:
- Use topic sentences and linking phrases to improve coherence.
- Keep sentences clear and avoid overly complex structures if accuracy suffers.
