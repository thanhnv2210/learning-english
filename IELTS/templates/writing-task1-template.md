# Writing Task 1 Template (Tech-focused)

- Task: Summarize the main trends, make comparisons, and highlight significant data points.

Structure:
1. Introduction: Rephrase the task (1 sentence)
2. Overview: Main trends or overall comparison (1–2 sentences)
3. Specific details: Paragraph for key points and data comparisons
4. Minor details or exceptions

Timing and tips:
- Spend 5 minutes planning, 15 minutes writing.
- Use comparative language: "higher than", "rose sharply", "remained stable".
- Avoid copying sentences from the question; paraphrase.
